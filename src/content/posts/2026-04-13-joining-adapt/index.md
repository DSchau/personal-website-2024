I've decided to take a new job in leading the Product & Engineering teams at Adapt, a seed-stage startup that aims to be the AI-native operating system for teams. In this post, I'll enumerate my reasons for joining, what excites me about the product and the team, and what I'll be doing.

## Why Adapt?

Lots of reasons, but in my experience I've relied on my instincts just as much or moreso than any particular framework or way of thinking. With Adapt, there are a few things that genuinely excite me:

1. **Leadership team**. In interviewing and getting to know the team, I have a strong degree of trust, conviction, and excitement about joining that team which includes Jim (CEO), Sean (CTO), and Ash (CMO).
2. **Autonomous execution**. Working with a business-focused CEO enables me to do what I do best: build great products and great teams to build them. I'm excited to build.
3. **AI-native**. All companies talk the talk about being AI-native, but joining a seed-stage startup that clearly embraces the future of how teams, products, and processes will be built will be immensely fun and I'm excited to learn more.

In general, in the interview loop I felt trusted, respected, and valued. Chatting with Jim and seeing his conviction and using the product made me excited. Chatting with Ash (who I worked with before and LOVED working with at Gatsby) and seeing how she's using the product, and then finally chatting with Sean.
